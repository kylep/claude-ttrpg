# Repo refactor pass (2026-07-15)

Driven by a five-part audit of the engine (core mechanics, CLI/infra,
render/export/viewer, content systems, and the test suite). This records
what got fixed and what was deliberately deferred. Items marked ✅ are done
in this pass; ⏭ are documented-and-deferred (added to `backlog.md`).

## Correctness

- ✅ **Spell attacks never doubled damage on a natural 20.** `combat.attack`
  doubles damage dice on a crit; `spells.cast`'s attack branch computed the
  hit but rolled plain damage — a real bug (spell crits under-damaged).
  Fixed by extracting shared `combat.resolve_hit()` and `combat.roll_damage()`
  and using them in both. Regression test added.

## Robustness (raw tracebacks → clean JSON errors)

- ✅ **`char create --assign DEX=--15`** passed the digit check then threw an
  uncaught `ValueError` (it wasn't wrapped by `guard`). Now validated with a
  strict `-?\d+` match and raised as `EngineError`.
- ✅ **`char create` with an all-symbol name** ("!!!") slugged to `pc-` and
  was silently accepted. Now guarded like `quest offer` already guards titles.
- ✅ **`equip`/`unequip` on a monster id** hit an uncaught `KeyError` (monsters
  have no `inventory`). Now a clean `EngineError("not_a_pc", …)`.
- ✅ **`quest offer --deadline-hour 99`** produced a silently-wrong deadline.
  Hour is now range-checked `0–23`.

## Deduplication (behavior-preserving)

- ✅ **`resolve_hit` / `roll_damage`** shared by `combat.attack` and
  `spells.cast` (see Correctness).
- ✅ **`reveal_actor`** — the "strip hidden, drop stealth, log, persist"
  sequence existed three times (`combat._reveal_actor`, inline in
  `combat.attack`, `spells._reveal_caster`). Now one public helper.
- ✅ **`check_reach`** — range + line-of-sight validation was copy-pasted in
  `combat.attack` and both `spells.cast` branches. One helper now.
- ✅ **`same_plane`** — the aloft-mismatch check lived in `combat.attack`,
  `combat._require_grabbable`, and `inventory.use`. One helper now.
- ✅ **`require_root_and_game()`** replaces the `root = require_root(); g =
  guard(worldfs.load_game_for, root)` pair repeated at 14 CLI sites.
- ✅ **`split_csv`** replaces three near-identical comma-splitters
  (`split_pcs`, quest `item_list`, override `actor_list`) — and fixes the
  tracked `--actors` whitespace bug in passing.
- ✅ **`parse_xy`** replaces duplicated `X,Y` parsing in `cast` and `move`.
- ✅ **`is_dead(sheet)`** in `derive.py` replaces the `"dead" in {e["name"]
  …}` idiom copy-pasted across `level`, `quests`, `rest`, `travel`.
- ✅ **`grant_xp_to(recipients, …)`** — `quests._grant_xp_scoped` was an
  acknowledged copy of `level.grant_xp`; now both share one function.
- ✅ **`hit_die_gain()`** shared by `level.up` and `rest.take`.
- ✅ **`slugify`** shared by `chargen` and `quests`.
- ✅ **`render_markdown`** — `story.py` reached into `export._md` (a private
  name). The markdown render+sanitize pipeline now lives in
  `markdown_render.py`, imported by both.

## Tests

- ✅ Replaced trivially-true map assertions (the static legend always
  contains `#`/`~`) with real grid-content checks in `test_e2e` and
  `test_grid_render`.
- ✅ Strengthened `test_export` digit assertions from bare substring matches
  to context-anchored checks.
- ✅ Added the coverage gaps the backlog named: `bad_coord` (move/cast),
  `check --adv/--dis` and crit/fumble, `grant_xp` dead-PC skip, and the
  untested `chargen` error paths (`unknown_class/race`, `exists`, bad assign).

## Deferred (moved to backlog.md)

- ⏭ **Split `combat.py` (908 LOC) into a `combat/` package** along its
  natural seams (encounter / attack / effects / flight / stealth / grapple /
  movement), re-exporting from `__init__` so callers don't change. High
  maintainability value but a large diff best done with review, not
  unattended. The in-place dedup above already removes the worst repetition.
- ⏭ **Split `cli.py` (584 LOC) into per-domain command modules**; also the
  `equip`/`unequip` vs `item …` grouping inconsistency — left alone because
  moving them under `item` would break the documented `engine equip` surface.
- ⏭ **`guard()` catches only `EngineError`/`ValueError`.** Broadening it to
  map `KeyError`/`TypeError` from hand-edited YAML into a `bad_state`
  envelope is a deliberate policy call.
- ⏭ Lower-value nits recorded but not actioned: `game.validate` split,
  `clock.advance` `divmod`, export card/optional-file helper extraction,
  `hostile/ally` iteration consolidation, hoisting the remaining transcript
  magic strings in `story.py`, and escaping the numeric export fields /
  `renders/index.html` filenames for consistency (trusted-operator threat
  model, low risk).
